Frequently Asked Questions

You have questions. We have answers.

Is Wasfa Gul Foundation a registered non-profit organisation?
Yes. The Foundation operates as a registered not-for-profit organisation committed to transparency and public benefit.
How are donations used?
Donations are allocated toward healthcare, education, infrastructure development and community welfare projects in accordance with the Foundation's objectives. Detailed reporting is shared in our annual updates.
Can I sponsor a patient or student?
Yes. The Foundation offers sponsorship opportunities for medical treatment and educational assistance. You can specify a preference when donating, or write to us for a tailored arrangement.
Can corporations partner with the Foundation?
Absolutely. We welcome CSR partnerships, sponsorships and collaborative projects with organisations that share our vision. Please get in touch via the contact form above to discuss opportunities.
Is my Zakat eligible to be given to the Foundation?
Yes — Zakat-eligible donations are tracked separately and used strictly for purposes that satisfy Zakat requirements. Please select "Zakat" when making your donation.
How can I receive updates from the Foundation?
Subscribe to our newsletter using the form in the footer. We share hospital progress, success stories and event invitations regularly.
