State-of-the-Art Hospital

Bringing advanced healthcare to Jhelum.

Our objective is to ensure that financial hardship never becomes a barrier to receiving quality medical treatment.

The Jhelum hospital is designed as a comprehensive facility serving the entire region, with equipment and clinical standards comparable to leading private hospitals.

  • ⛑Emergency Services
  • 🩺Outpatient Clinics
  • 🛏Inpatient Facilities

  • ❤Intensive Care
