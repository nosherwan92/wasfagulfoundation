Asnaf-e-Zakat

The eight categories of eligible recipients

Zakat is distributed strictly to the recipients identified in Surah At-Tawbah, verse 60. The Foundation's eligible programmes serve recipients in categories marked below.

✓

The poor (Fuqara)

Patients and families unable to meet basic needs.

✓

The needy (Masakin)

Those whose means cannot cover medical or educational expenses.

○

Zakat administrators

Not used by the Foundation — administration is funded separately.

○

Those whose hearts are to be reconciled

Not a category we operate in.

○

Freeing captives

Not a category we operate in.

✓

Those in debt (Gharimeen)

Patients with medical debt may qualify under this category.

✓

In the cause of Allah (Fi Sabilillah)

Education programmes for deserving students may qualify under this category.

✓

The wayfarer (Ibn as-Sabil)

Stranded patients travelling for medical care.
