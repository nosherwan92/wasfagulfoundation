About the Foundation

Building lasting institutions, not temporary relief.

Wasfa Gul Foundation is a registered non-profit established to address two of the most fundamental human needs — healthcare and education — through sustainable, transparent initiatives.

Millions of families in Pakistan struggle to access quality medical facilities. Countless talented students are unable to pursue education because of economic barriers. We are building permanent institutions that will serve generations to come.

Chairman's Message

"We build hope, restore dignity, and create opportunity."

Every great nation is built upon healthy and educated citizens. Many families continue to suffer because quality healthcare and education remain beyond their financial reach — and Wasfa Gul Foundation was established to change this reality.

Our vision extends beyond constructing buildings. We aim to build hope, restore dignity, and create opportunities for those who need them most. We invite individuals, businesses, philanthropists and development partners to join us.

Dr Khalid Gondal
Chairman · Wasfa Gul Foundation
