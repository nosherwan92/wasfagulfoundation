Sadaqah with Wasfa Gul Foundation

Give whenever your heart moves you.

Sadaqah is voluntary charity — distinct from the obligatory Zakat. There is no minimum threshold, no fixed percentage, no eligibility test.

Your Sadaqah supports the Foundation's full programme: hospital construction, medical equipment, free treatment for patients who don't qualify under Zakat's eight categories, student scholarships, and community welfare initiatives.

Unlike Zakat, Sadaqah may be used for both programmes and operating costs — administrative, marketing and outreach work that keeps the Foundation running.

Why give Sadaqah?

  • ✓No threshold — give any amount, any time
  • ✓Wider use — supports programmes and operations
  • ✓Recurring option — set up a monthly Sadaqah for sustained impact
  • ✓Sadaqah Jariyah — equipment and infrastructure gifts that keep benefiting people for years
  • ✓In memory of a loved one — dedicate your Sadaqah to a relative or friend

Sadaqah vs Zakat

Understanding the difference

Zakat

  • ⚖Obligatory on Muslims meeting Nisab
  • ⚖Fixed at 2.5% of eligible wealth
  • ⚖Annual
  • ⚖Strictly for 8 categories of recipients
  • ⚖Cannot fund admin overhead

Sadaqah

  • ✦Voluntary for anyone
  • ✦Any amount
  • ✦Any time
  • ✦For any beneficial cause
  • ✦Can support programmes and operations
