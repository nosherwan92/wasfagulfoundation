State-of-the-Art Hospital, Jhelum

A modern charitable hospital designed to provide quality healthcare to underserved communities.

Why this hospital matters

Bringing world-class care within reach.

Financial hardship should never determine whether a person receives quality medical treatment.

In and around Jhelum, thousands of families travel long distances or postpone urgent care because they cannot afford private hospital fees. The Foundation's flagship hospital is designed to close that gap — combining modern equipment, qualified staff, and a charitable mission.

This is not a clinic. It is a comprehensive, multi-speciality facility intended to become a long-term anchor for healthcare in the region.
